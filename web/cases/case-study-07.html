Kellogg's

Who: Kellogg's

What: Create awareness in Greater Manchester area that Kellogg's still recruit locally and not just nationally/globally.

How: We recommended a Fish4Jobs campaign which included listings, leaderboard ads and ‘Featured Recruiter’ positions.

Feedback: Kellogg’s were delighted when the campaign resulted in over 1,200 views over three roles in one month.
"We were really happy with the visibility in the local area and impressed with the number of links through to our own website."
