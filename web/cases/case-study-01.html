Aldi

Who: Aldi

What: Maintain recruitment levels in existing stores and grow the workforce by 5,000 during 2015 to aid the opening of 70 new stores. New personnel to include 70 store managers, 150 assistant store managers and 4,500 stock and store assistants.

How: Fish4jobs - five national listings used supported by homepage inventory on the national site and regional hubs.

Feedback: In the first three weeks, the campaign generated 45,867 views and 21,302 clicked through to apply. This level of activity and the 46% conversion is unprecedented and is a 33% increase in views and apps on the same campaign that ran in October of last year.
